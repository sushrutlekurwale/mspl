About Us

We are pleased to share that Meeta Screws Pvt. Ltd. is a leading manufacturing company in Fasteners, We manufacture fasteners in various shapes, profiles and dimensional accuracy made out of Mild Steel, Low and Medium Carbon Steel, Aluminium Alloys and Stainless Steel as per customner specifications. We manufacture Screws, Bolts, Rivets, Turned and Forged Fasteners, Screw/Bolt Assemblies, Hex & Flange Nuts, Washers. We have best suited surface treatment plant for Fasteners with facility for Rohs complaint trivalent passivations with Alkaline & Iron Zink Plating. High Tensile fasteners are manufactured using high quality alloy steels, best machinery and innovative technology and highly skilled workforce to ensure zero defect quality. We have more than 17 years experience in the fasteners manufacturing. Our products are well appreciated and accepted all over India, Our prestigious clientele are from Automobile, Electrical, Electronics, Furniture and Construction industries across India.
